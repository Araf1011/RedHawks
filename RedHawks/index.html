RedHawks is a premier Free Fire esports team from Bangladesh, born from a passion for gaming and a drive to dominate the battlefield. Founded in 2025, we represent the spirit of Bangladesh, blending skill, strategy, and teamwork to compete at the highest levels. Join us as we aim for global glory!
